## Pricing rules

- **Monthly:** list price, billed monthly.
- **Annual:** 15% off — effective monthly = `list × 0.85`; annual total = `list × 12 × 0.85`.
- **Founder Members (first 50 clients only) — BASE PACKAGES ONLY:**
  - Eligible plans: **Launchpad** (Home Services), **Glow Engine** (Med Spa), **Fetch** (Pet Grooming). Higher tiers are **not** eligible.
  - **Founder Monthly:** 70% off list monthly = `list × 0.30`.
  - **Founder Annual:** 80% off list annual = `list × 12 × 0.20`; effective monthly = `list × 0.20`.
  - **Term:** the Founder rate is valid for **one year**, then the plan **reverts to the standard rate** at renewal. State this in the promo fine print.
- Display rounding: round to the nearest whole dollar for display (figures below rounded).
- Med Spa and Pet Grooming list prices below are **confirmed**.

## Package contents

### Home Services
- **Launchpad:** Missed-call text-back · instant lead response (forms auto-reply in minutes) · appointment booking automation · automated Google review requests post-job · basic CRM (contacts + lead source) · monthly report. *No: unified inbox, visual pipeline, reminders, nurture, referral/reactivation, ads, GBP, custom workflows, strategy calls.*
- **Momentum:** Everything in Launchpad + unified inbox (SMS/email/calls/FB DMs) · visual lead & job pipeline · appointment reminders · GBP optimization · basic single-platform ad setup (Google OR Meta) · email & SMS nurture · referral automation · reactivation (6–12mo inactive). *No: full ad management, advanced nurture, custom workflows, seasonal campaigns, reputation domination, strategy call.*
- **Pinnacle:** Everything in Momentum + full Google & Meta ad management · advanced multi-step nurture w/ segmentation · end-to-end workflow automation (contact→booked→invoice→re-booking) · seasonal campaign automation · reputation domination · advanced segmented reactivation & referral · custom workflows · monthly strategy call.

### Med Spa
- **Glow Engine:** Missed-call text-back · instant response to web/IG/FB consult inquiries · online booking + automated appointment reminders (cut no-shows) · automated review requests post-visit · basic client CRM (contact + visit/treatment tracking + source) · monthly report.
- **Glow Authority:** Everything in Glow Engine + unified inbox (SMS/email/IG & FB DMs) · rebooking & treatment-cycle reminders (e.g. Botox 3-mo, facials monthly) · membership & package follow-up · email & SMS nurture for consult leads · lapsed-client reactivation · Google & Meta ad management (consult/promo lead gen) · seasonal promo campaigns (wedding season, holidays, Mother's Day) · reputation management · monthly strategy call.

### Pet Grooming
- **Fetch:** Missed-call text-back · instant response to booking inquiries · online booking + automated reminders (reduce no-shows) · automated review requests post-groom · basic client & pet CRM (owner + pet, breed/service, source) · monthly report.
- **Best in Show:** Everything in Fetch + unified inbox · recurring grooming reminders on cycle ("Buddy's due for a groom," ~6–8 weeks) · loyalty/package follow-up · email & SMS nurture for new inquiries · lapsed-client reactivation · Google & Meta ad management · seasonal campaigns (shedding season, summer, holidays) · reputation management · monthly strategy call.

> Anything outside a package's scope (e.g. standalone website design, video ad creative, ongoing SEO content) is available as a separate add-on. Med Spa & Pet "premium" tiers include marketing management; "base" tiers are automation-only.
